About Me.

Allow me to introduce myself

I 'm Kushal Borkar, currently an undergrad from IIIT SriCity, born and raised in Kolkata, city of joy. I began by exploring some fascinating and interesting projects with regards to Image Processing and finally in due course subtly shifted to Machine Learning, Analytics and Statistics almost a year prior.
Every so often, while considered not occupied with academics or work, I set aside out some time for reading a novel or a blog post, playing DoTA2, watching anime or tuning in to podcasts.
I am still figuring out what I am doing with my life. But hey, nice to meet you!
